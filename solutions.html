3-D Mapping

A robust and dynamic 3-D mapping solution for charting the path of a first responder as they traverse through harsh and uncertain environments. This technology is capable of handling complex and evolving conditions, specifically in GPS denied environments.

Real Time Location Intelligence

Real-time analysis, visualization and tracking of safety personnel for enhanced situational awareness. This technology integrates location data with infrastructure data to discover swift exit strategies.

Multi-modal Data Fusion

A synergistic combination of information simultaneously acquired from various types of sensors of the same environment. This information can include visible images, thermal images, 3D maps, audio, vitals, airpack levels, and pre-existing maps.

Real-time Image Enhancement

A cutting-edge solution to improve the quality and information content of digital images to enhance human interpretation. Images can include data acquired from visible and thermal sensors that are affected by environmental conditions such as smoke, fire, and rain. This AI powered algorithm can be used to remove these distortions and  improve image quality and resolution.
