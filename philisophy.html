First responders risk their lives every day while protecting and aiding people at the scene of an emergency. But, did you know that the current technology used by our first responders is inadequate to safeguard them from dangerous situations? We at Tessera Intelligence are creating a suite of revolutionary artificial intelligence-based products that will bring efficiency and accuracy to mission-critical decisions of first responders. Tessera Intelligence is developing a dynamic real-time 3D mapping and tracking technology to monitor the location of our first responders. This technology coupled with our image processing expertise will enable them to function in conditions such as fog, smoke, and fire. We are also building a health monitoring system to track vitals of first responders and a communication system to rapidly relay data from the first responders to the command center.
